Ingredients:

  • 1 (10-12 lb) turkey
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp (30 mL) miso paste
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) minced garlic
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) ground cumin
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp (2 mL) cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper for taste

Instructions

  1. Combine Glaze: In a small bowl, whisk together maple syrup, soy sauce, miso paste, ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  2. Prepare Turkey: Rinse the turkey inside and out. Pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Glaze: Place the turkey in a large roasting pan. Pour the glaze over the turkey, rubbing it all over the skin.
  4. Roast: Roast the turkey in a preheated oven at 325Â°F (165Â°C) for about 2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F (74Â°C). Baste the turkey with the pan juices every 30 minutes.
  5. Rest: Let the turkey rest for about 20 minutes before carving.
